Booking

If you want to book the Cat's Eye for an event, follow the following steps. Once you're done, fill out our booking form bellow.

  • Check Availability

    Take a look at out calendar[link to calendar?] and check that your desired date and time aren't taken. It's also a good idea to consider whether there are similar events happening around that time.

  • Check Equipment

    If your event requires any special equipment, make sure that we have it! A list of our equipment is provided above in our SPACE[link] section. Some equipment such as podiums, additional projectors or chairs must be requested through Events Victoria ([insert mailto link]).

  • Book the Bar

    If you'd like the bar to be open during your event, you need to book it separately along with security for the duration of the event. To do so, contact Events Victoria and fill out a risk assessment form. Once you have booked the space and the bar, forward the confirmation email to us. Note: food must be provided by the organisers when booking a bar event.
