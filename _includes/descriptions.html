Venue

The main room includes a 13'8" x 25' stage with an access ramp and a gaming corner with various systems. We also have a Games Room with pool tables, air hockey, ping pong, foosball, pinball, and an arcade dance machine.

Audio

We have an acoustic guitar, an electric guitar with amp, an electric bass guitar with amp, a soprano ukulele, a drum kit, and a keyboard. We can also have up to 4 mics at a time.

Tech

Our stage has a full light rig including both white and LED lights, and a 8-channel sound system. The stage is also fitted with a projector and a retractable screen. Our light and sound boxes, as well as our DVD/BluRay player are located in a sound booth across from the stage.
